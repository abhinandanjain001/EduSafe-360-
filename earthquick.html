Google AI Studio Prompts for EduSafe 360

Tip: Copy these prompts into Google AI Studio to generate content for your disaster management website.

Chatbot Training Prompts

Disaster Response Expert Persona

Act as an emergency response expert specializing in disaster preparedness and safety. Your name is EduSafe Assistant. You provide clear, actionable advice for various disaster scenarios including earthquakes, floods, fires, and hurricanes. You are knowledgeable, reassuring, and always prioritize safety. You answer questions concisely but thoroughly, and when appropriate, ask follow-up questions to ensure the user's specific situation is addressed. You never provide medical advice beyond basic first aid, instead directing users to seek professional medical help when needed.
Use case: Setting the personality and expertise level for your chatbot

Flood Safety Dialogue

Create a dialogue between a flood safety expert and a concerned homeowner. The homeowner lives in a flood-prone area and is worried about upcoming heavy rains. The expert should provide specific advice on preparation, evacuation planning, emergency kits, and what to do during a flood. Include questions about the home's location, existing preparations, and the homeowner's specific concerns. The dialogue should be educational but conversational, with the expert reassuring while providing critical safety information.
Use case: Generating training data for your flood safety chatbot

VR Simulation Content

Flood VR Simulation Script

Create a script for a virtual reality flood safety simulation. The simulation should have three phases: 1) Preparation (recognizing warning signs, gathering supplies), 2) Response (evacuation decisions, navigating floodwaters), and 3) Aftermath (safety assessment, helping others). Include dialogue for an AI guide that provides instructions, feedback, and educational information throughout the experience. The script should include visual descriptions, interactive elements, and safety messages that align with FEMA and Red Cross guidelines.
Use case: Generating content for your Genei 3 VR flood simulation

Earthquake Drill Dialogue

Generate a script for an interactive VR earthquake drill. The scenario should take place in a school setting. Include instructions from a teacher, responses from students, and feedback from a safety expert narrator. Cover the key actions: Drop, Cover, and Hold On; evacuation procedures; and accountability measures. Include common mistakes people make during earthquakes and corrective guidance. Make the dialogue realistic and appropriate for different age groups.
Use case: Creating realistic dialogue for VR safety drills

Educational Content Generation

Disaster Preparedness Guide

Create a comprehensive disaster preparedness guide for families. The guide should cover: creating a family emergency plan, building emergency kits for home and vehicles, identifying safe spaces in different types of homes for various disasters, establishing communication plans, and special considerations for children, elderly family members, and pets. Organize the information clearly with headings, bullet points, and actionable steps. Include checklists for supplies and important documents to secure.
Use case: Generating educational content for your website's resource section

Interactive Quiz Questions

Generate 20 multiple-choice questions about disaster preparedness and response. Include questions about earthquakes, floods, fires, hurricanes, and tornadoes. For each question, provide 4 options with one correct answer and detailed explanations for both correct and incorrect answers. The questions should test practical knowledge and common misconceptions about disaster safety. Format the questions clearly with designations for the correct answer.
Use case: Creating interactive learning content for your website

API Integration Prompts

Chatbot Response Template

Create a JSON template for a disaster response chatbot. The template should include fields for: disaster_type, safety_steps (array of steps), immediate_actions, common_mistakes, emergency_contacts, and additional_resources. For each disaster type (flood, earthquake, fire, hurricane, tornado), generate example content that follows this template. The responses should be concise, actionable, and suitable for display on a website chat interface.
Use case: Structuring responses for your AI-powered chatbot

Emergency Scenario Generator

Create a function that generates random emergency scenarios for training purposes. The function should output a JSON object with: scenario_type, location, severity_level, environmental_conditions, available_resources, and time_constraint. Write example scenarios for different disaster types with varying difficulty levels. Include both common situations and rare but critical scenarios that require specialized knowledge.
Use case: Creating dynamic training scenarios for your VR simulations
Implementation Tip: After generating content in Google AI Studio, you can use the API to integrate these responses directly into your EduSafe 360 website. Make sure to review and refine all AI-generated content for accuracy before deployment.
